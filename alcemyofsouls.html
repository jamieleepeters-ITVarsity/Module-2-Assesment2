Alcemy of Souls

Alchemy of Souls (Korean: 환혼; Hanja: 還魂; RR: Hwanhon) is a South Korean television series starring Lee Jae-wook, Jung So-min, and Hwang Min-hyun.[9] Written by the Hong sisters, it depicts the stories of young mages dealing with heaven and earth.[10][11] It premiered on tvN on June 18, 2022, and aired every Saturday and Sunday at 21:10 (KST).[12] It is also available for streaming on TVING and Netflix in selected regions.[13] Age restriction: All
